The Main Character: You

You are the main character of our story. You love natural because you care about your family and friends. You want to make sure that the world and home you make for them right now is a natural one and the one you leave behind for the next generation is better than the one now. Because of this, you want to know what is in the products you use. You don't just want it to say that it is natural, but you want to know that each and every ingredient is natural. It isn't about you, it is about caring for others.

The Problem

Essential oils cost too much. You hate bringing in unnatural products into your home and apartment, but when faced with the choice between natural and your wallet, you often have a difficult choice. Do you take the risk on the harmful effects artificial products could have in order to make ends meet? Or do you sacrifice your wallet for natural products? And on top of it all, all these so called natural companies and just greenwashing and charging extra just because they throw a green label on it.

Meet Simply Earth

We have been faced with the choice too many times in our past between natural and our wallet. We were afraid of the harmful effects artificial products could have, but also didn’t have thousands of dollars to completely make our home natural. We are mothers, fathers, and friends. Having been extensively trained in oils, we made a natural shoe odor eliminator ourselves and started selling it. Some of the key ingredients in our odor eliminator were essential oils and when we were buying them in bulk from a high quality supplier, we realized quality essential oils don’t have to break the bank, nor does making your home natural.

Essential oils cost too much, so we fixed it.

After realizing we can get high quality essential oils for ½ the price the big oil companies were making us pay, we dug in deep to make sure we could outmatch their quality while keeping prices low. We source our oils from farms all around the world, wherever the oil grows best. We bottle them in-house, to control quality. We double test everything to make sure it is pesticide free and pure so you can enjoy natural products for a price that does not break your wallet.
